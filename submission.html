Submission Guidelines

Papers must be submitted through the CMT platform.

The maximum number of pages is 10 + 2 for references only, using the LNCS proceedings format.

All submissions will go through a double-blind review process. Papers will be selected based on relevance, significance and novelty of results, technical merit, and clarity of presentation.

Each accepted paper must be covered by at least one author registration (either a Full registration or a Workshop/Tutorial registration, if you plan to attend the workshops/tutorials only).
